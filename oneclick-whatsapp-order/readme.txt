== Description ==

### Speed up the purchase process. Increase your sales!

Formerly known as "OneClick WhatsApp Order", **OneClick Chat to Order** will make it easier for your customers to order your products directly through WhatsApp with a single click. Connect your WooCommerce-powered online store with WhatsApp and make it super quick and easy for your customers to complete their order via WhatsApp.
